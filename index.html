Why Shiba Inu Token (SHIBA)

SHIBA is a very popular cryptocurrency in the crypto community and has a high level of liquidity on the world's leading crypto exchanges. However, the value of SHIBA is also very volatile and can change in a short period of time. To increase the attractiveness of SHIBA, staking and burning features have been applied to this cryptocurrency. Staking SHIBA allows users to hold their SHIBA for a certain period of time and receive rewards in the form of SHIBA or other cryptocurrencies. This is a way to attract users to hold SHIBA and increase the liquidity of this cryptocurrency. For the burn feature, a percentage of SHIBA is kept and used to buy and burn crypto coins. different in the market. SHIBA burn rate is set quarterly, i.e. 10% of SHIBA is burned at the end of each quarter. Burning SHIBA reduces the supply of SHIBA available in the market, thereby creating scarcity and helping to increase the value of the cryptocurrency. $SHIBA?
